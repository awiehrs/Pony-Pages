Perfect Ponies


Ponies are sweet. They have tiny little hooves that hold up their perfect little legs. On top of their legs are their fat little bodies. They have a mane, a tail, and an unusually large head with a mouth full of horribly disgusting rectangle teeth. But that's not all! They smell like grass, mud, and poop, too!
They can sometimes love to cuddle as well. But you must befriend them, first. Or else.....
